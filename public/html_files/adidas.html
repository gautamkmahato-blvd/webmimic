Dominant Color Note
#000000 pure black appears 13,460 times — the single most-used color token across the entire series of 23 systems. The adidas product catalog, imagery backgrounds, and UI surfaces are overwhelmingly black. No accent color is defined at the brand level; the fill colors are exclusively social platform brand colors (Facebook, Twitter, TikTok, Google).
