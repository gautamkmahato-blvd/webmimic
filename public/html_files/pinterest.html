Z-Index
7 layers — 1→2→3→4→5 then a dramatic jump to 671 and 1500. The z=1 through z=5 cluster handles Pinterest's masonry layout ordering. The jump to 671 is for the top navigation/sticky header overlay. z=1500 is the highest-priority modal layer (shopping cart, full-screen Pin viewer).
